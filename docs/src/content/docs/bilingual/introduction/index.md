第一章

> 1 INTRODUCTION

歡迎來到歸零年代。本文件包含桌遊角色扮演用的核心 Year Zero Engine（YZE）機制，被多款 Free League 遊戲所採用。

> Welcome to Year Zero. This document contains the core Year Zero Engine mechanics for tabletop roleplaying, used in sever- al Free League games.

**基礎概念**　本節介紹角色扮演的幾個關鍵概念，以及它們在使用 Year Zero Engine 的遊戲中如何運作。

> THE BASICS This section introduces some key concepts in roleplaying, and how they are used in games using the Year Zero Engine.

**玩家**　除一人外，每位玩家各自扮演一個玩家角色（PC）。你決定你的 PC 思考什麼、感受什麼、說什麼、做什麼——但無法決定發生在他身上的事。你的職責是投入你的 PC，讓自己沉浸其中。他可能是遙遠奇幻世界的冒險者，但在內心深處，他仍是一個有情感與夢想的人，就像你一樣。試著想像：如果你身處他的處境，你會有何反應？你會怎麼做？玩家角色永遠是故事的主角。這個遊戲是關於你的——你的決定，你的冒險。

> THE PLAYERS Each player except one controls a player character (PC). You decide what your PC thinks and feels, what they say and do – but not what happens to them. It is your job as a player to immerse yourself in your PC. They may be an adventurer in a faraway fantasy world – but they are still, at heart, a person with feelings and dreams, just like you. Try to imagine – how would you react if you were in their shoes? What would you do? The player characters are always the protagonists of the story. The game is about you. Your decisions, your adventures.

**遊戲主持人**　最後一位玩家是遊戲主持人（GM）。他向你描述遊戲世界，扮演你遇到的人物，並操控你要對抗的敵人。遊戲是玩家與 GM 之間來回的對話，直到出現結果不確定的關鍵時刻。這時就要拿出骰子——詳見第三章。

> THE GAMEMASTER The final player is the Gamemaster, the GM. They describe the game world to you, they portray the people you meet, and they control the enemies you fight. The game is a conversation between the players and the GM, back and forth, until a critical situation arises where the outcome is uncertain. Then it's time to break out the dice – read more about this in Chapter 3.

GM 的職責是在你的路上設置障礙並挑戰你的 PC，迫使他們展現真正的實力。但 GM 無法決定遊戲中發生的一切——尤其是你的故事將如何結束。那由遊戲本身決定。這就是你玩這個遊戲的原因——為了找出你的故事如何終結。

> It is the GM's job to put obstacles in your path and challenge your PCs, forcing them to show what they're real- ly made of. But it is not up to the GM to decide everything that happens in the game – and above all, not how your story is supposed to end. That is decided in the game. That is why you are playing the game – to find out how your story ends.

**核心特色**　Year Zero Engine 最初是為《Mutant: Year Zero》開發的規則系統，後來被進一步修改並適用於各種不同主題與背景的遊戲。儘管如此，遊戲的六項核心特色在所有版本中始終如一，列舉並說明如下。

> KEY FEATURES The Year Zero Engine was originally developed as the ruleset for Mutant: Year Zero, but has been further modified and adapted to a wide range of games with different themes and settings. Yet, six core features of the game remain the same in all iterations. These are listed and explained below.

**易於上手**　Year Zero Engine 的基礎非常容易學習，也很容易教給新玩家，讓入門門檻極低。複雜度與深度逐步遞增，隨著玩家對系統的理解加深，提供更多選擇。

> ACCESSIBLE The basics of the Year Zero Engine are very easy to learn. It is easy to teach to new players, making the barrier to play very low. Complexity and depth are added piece by piece, offering more choices to the player as they gain more insight into the system.

**快速果決**　Year Zero Engine 節奏迅速，去除不推動行動的擲骰、記帳與計算，快速得出有意義的結果。YZE 的戰鬥系統往往相當致命，將衝突推向決定性時刻。風險極高，無論 PC 多有經驗，都很少能夠免於危險。

> FAST AND DECISIVE The Year Zero Engine is fast, quickly producing meaningful results by removing any dice rolling, bookkeeping and calcu- lations that don't move the action forward. Year Zero Engine combat systems are often deadly, pushing conflicts to decisive moments. The risks are high, and PCs are rarely safe from danger no matter how experienced they are.

**風險與回報**　在 Year Zero Engine 中，你可以透過強推擲骰——即重新擲骰——大幅提升成功機率，但強推必然伴隨代價。這種動態機制讓你不斷權衡風險與回報，也使 YZE 特別適合嚴酷、求生為核心的遊戲。

> RISKS & REWARDS In the Year Zero Engine, you can increase your chances signifi- cantly by pushing your roll – i.e. re-rolling the dice – but push- ing always comes with a cost. This dynamic constantly pushes you to weigh risks and rewards, and makes the Year Zero Engine particularly suited for harsh, survival-focused games.

**以玩家為中心**　在 Year Zero Engine 的遊戲中，玩家及其角色是故事的核心。PC 是故事的主角，而非 NPC。規則聚焦於 PC 及其行動，而 GM 則快速有效地處理 NPC。這個系統設計上始終為玩家提供有意義的選擇。

> PLAYER-CENTRIC In Year Zero Engine games, the players and their characters are at the heart of the story. The PCs are the protagonists of the story, never the NPCs. The rules focus on the PCs and their actions, while NPCs are handled quickly and effectively by the Gamemaster. The system is designed to always present the players with meaningful choices.

**故事驅動**　角色扮演是關於創造故事，是那些在桌旁留下的難忘時刻，讓你多年後仍記憶猶新。Year Zero Engine 的設計旨在產生戲劇性效果，推動你的故事向前發展並帶來意想不到的轉折。

> STORY DRIVEN Roleplaying is about creating stories, memorable moments at the gaming table that you'll remember for years to come. The Year Zero Engine is designed to produce dramatic effects that will push your story forward and make it take unexpected turns.

**擲骰**　規則有時會要求你擲 D3、2D6、D66 及 D100。D3 表示擲一顆 D6 並將結果除以二，無條件進位。2D6 表示擲兩顆六面骰並將結果相加。D66 表示擲兩顆 D6，第一顆代表十位數，第二顆代表個位數，結果介於 11 到 66 之間。D100 表示擲兩顆 D10，第一顆代表十位數，第二顆代表個位數，雙零計為 100。你甚至可以擲 D666，用三顆六面骰，第一顆計百位數，第二顆計十位數，第三顆計個位數。

**適應性強**　Year Zero Engine 設計為非常容易適應不同的遊戲風格、主題與背景。透過模組化的方式使用技能與天賦，系統形成積木式的建構單元，非常容易新增、移除與改造。

> ADAPTABLE The Year Zero Engine is designed to be very adaptable for different play styles, themes, and game settings. By using skills and talents in a modular fashion, the system creates building blocks that are very easily added, removed, and re-engineered.

**自訂牌組**　YZE 遊戲的另一個實用配件是自訂牌組。這些牌可用作裝備或 NPC 的參考卡，也可用來在戰鬥中隨機決定先攻順序——詳見第四章。

> CUSTOM CARDS Another useful accessory for YZE games is a custom card deck. The cards can be used as reference sheets for gear or NPCs, but also to randomize initiative in combat – read more about this in Chapter 4.

**骰池與階骰**　每款 Year Zero 遊戲各有不同，但可以辨識出兩種主要分支——原始的骰池版本（使用多顆 D6）和較新的階骰版本（使用各種多面骰）。本 SRD 包含兩種版本。設計你的遊戲時，可以選擇任一偏好的版本。

> DICE POOLS vs STEP DICE Every Year Zero Game is different, but two main forks can be identified – the original dice pool variety (using pools of D6s) and the more recent step dice version (using a variety of polyhedral dice). Both versions are included in this SRD. When designing your game, you can choose whichever version you prefer.

**計量時間**　YZE 遊戲依情況使用三種時間單位（見右側表格）。輪次、片段與升階的確切時長可依情況有所不同。追蹤時間並判斷何時進入下一個輪次、片段或升階是 GM 的職責。一天通常有四個升階時段：早晨、白晝、傍晚與夜晚。

**角色卡**　用來記錄你的角色，你會使用角色卡。本文件不含角色卡，因為任何 YZE 遊戲都需要針對特定規則版本與遊戲背景設計專屬的角色卡。如何建立你的角色將在下一章說明。

> CHARACTER SHEETS To document your character, you use a character sheet. This document does not include a character sheet, as any YZE game will need a sheet adapted to the specific rules version and game setting. How you create your character will be described in the next chapter.

**骰子**　作為 Year Zero Engine 遊戲中的角色，你將不得不承擔風險。遲早，無論你多有技能，你都會陷入結果不確定的情境。這時就要拿出骰子。骰池版 YZE 需要普通的六面骰（也稱 D6），最好備有 10–15 顆；而階骰版 YZE 則使用多面骰，包括 D8、D10 和 D12。某些規則變體需要不同顏色的骰子。

> DICE As a character in a Year Zero Engine game, you will have to take risks. Sooner or later, you will end up in situations where the outcome is uncertain, no matter how skilled you are. It's time to break out the dice. Regular six-sided dice (also called D6) are required to play the dice pool version YZE, preferably 10–15 of them, while the step dice version of YZE uses poly- hedral dice, including D8, D10 and D12. Some rules variations require dice in different colors.

**安全遊玩**　在 YZE 遊戲中，你在很大程度上掌控故事的走向，而這也帶來責任。玩家角色將面對巨大危險與艱難挑戰，但任何玩家都不應在體驗中感到不愉快或受到冒犯。確保桌上每個人都能盡興、感到安全，這一點至關重要。

> PLAYING SAFELY In YZE games, you are largely in control of the story, and with this comes responsibility. The player characters will face great danger and difficult challenges, but no player should find the situations they experience unpleasant or offensive. It is import- ant that everyone around the gaming table is having fun and feeling safe.

在開始遊戲前，請先溝通交流，看看是否有人希望將某些主題排除在遊戲之外。如果有玩家想暫停並討論遊戲中發生的事，甚至想離開桌子，請始終予以尊重。你可能也會想在遊戲結束後聊聊發生的一切。

> Before starting the game, talk things through and see if someone wants certain subjects to be kept out of the game. Al- ways respect a player who wants to pause and discuss what is happening in the game, or even leave the table if the player so chooses. And you may also want to talk about what happened after the game session.
